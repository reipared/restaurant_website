About Us

Delicious dishes with unique cultural twists

Forget flour power, at Forklore, we're all about mangú magic and plantain passion. Ditch the gluten, not the flavor, with our vibrant takes on Dominican classics. Sink your teeth into chimichurri-kissed grilled fish, light and flaky as the island breeze. Dive into creamy mangú bowls, whipped to fluffy perfection with secret spices. Mofongo mayhem takes center stage, a crispy dance of sweet plantains and veggie surprises. Every bite a fiesta, every plate a fiesta-less, your taste buds will conga line with joy at Forklore. Come savor the vibrant soul of Dominican cuisine, gluten-free and guilt-free!

Food Menu

Fried Plantain

Imagine golden sunshine captured in a crispy shell – that's a fried plantain. Picture slices of starchy green heaven, transformed by hot oil into irresistible wedges. Their exterior boasts a symphony of textures – crackly, airy, with hints of caramelized sweetness. Inside, a soft, creamy paradise awaits, infused with subtle earthiness and hints of banana's distant cousin. It's a culinary paradox, a dance between sweet and savory, light and substantial. Each bite is a journey, a reminder that healthy and delicious can tango on the same plate.

Price: $ 250

Mofongo

Picture a golden sun cast in a mortar and pestle, pounded into a savory, starchy symphony. That's mofongo, the crown jewel of Dominican cuisine. Green plantains, kissed by hot oil, transform into airy pillows, mashed with garlic's fiery whispers and chicharrón's salty crunch. Each bite is a paradox – light yet substantial, earthy yet vibrant, bursting with the island's essence. Mofongo's heart holds endless possibilities – succulent shrimp nestled in its embrace, creamy cheese swirling through its depths, or the tangy dance of fresh vegetables adding a playful twist. It's a blank canvas for culinary creativity, a dish that celebrates tradition while welcoming innovation. So dig in, savor the crunch, and let mofongo transport you to the heart of the Dominican Republic with every delicious mouthful.

Price: $ 250

Dominican Coffee

Picture this: sunrise over the Caribbean, casting a warm glow on fields of emerald-green coffee plants. That's the essence of Dominican coffee – sun-kissed and vibrant. Each sip is a journey, starting with a rich, caramelized aroma that promises adventure. Take a breath, and it unfolds into a symphony of flavors – hints of dark chocolate, whispers of toasted nuts, and a touch of sweet citrus dancing on the edges. The body is full, warm, and smooth, like a gentle wave rolling onto the shore. But don't be fooled by its mellow character – there's a subtle kick, a whisper of cinnamon and spice that lingers long after the last sip. Dominican coffee isn't just a drink, it's an experience – a taste of the island's soul, brewed with sunshine and steeped in tradition. So close your eyes, embrace the warmth, and let Dominican coffee transport you to paradise.

Price: $ 250

Fried Fish

Picture the Caribbean sun glistening off golden scales, then imagine those scales transformed into a symphony of crisp and flaky perfection. That's Dominican fried fish. Each bite is a party: a crackle of crispy batter gives way to tender, melt-in-your-mouth white fish, infused with the vibrant warmth of garlic, oregano, and adobo. The ocean's essence whispers in every morsel, with a hint of lime dancing on the edges. Forget fish sticks – this is a tropical explosion, a taste of island life served piping hot with a squeeze of lime and a drizzle of spicy mayo. So dive in, let the crunch serenade your ears, and let the flavors transport you to paradise with every bite.

Price: $ 250

Habichuelas con Dulce

Forget your preconceived notions of beans – Dominican habichuelas con dulce is a creamy, soul-stirring dance between sweet and savory. Imagine velvety red kidney beans, simmered slow with coconut milk and evaporated milk, until they melt into a symphony of smooth richness. Spices like cinnamon and cloves whisper warmth, while the sweetness of raisins and a hint of rum add a playful wink. It's a dessert that defies expectations, a comforting embrace in a bowl, as decadent as it is nourishing. Each spoonful is a celebration, a taste of Dominican tradition that lingers long after the last bite. So open your heart, grab a spoon, and let habichuelas con dulce serenade your taste buds with its island charm.

Price: $ 250

La Bandera

Imagine a plate singing the Dominican national anthem with every bite – that's La Bandera. Picture fluffy white rice, a blank canvas kissed by the sun, cradling a rich, ruby tide of stewed red beans. Each spoonful ripples with savory notes, whispers of garlic and oregano dancing with the vibrant pulse of kidney beans. On top, a melody of protein takes center stage – braised chicken, stewed beef, or tender pork, each adding its own harmonious verse. La Bandera isn't just food, it's a cultural chorus, a taste of history and home in every forkful. So close your eyes, listen to the symphony of flavors, and let La Bandera paint your palate with the soul of the Dominican Republic.

Price: $ 250
